I'm a prefinal year student at the Indian Institute of Technology Dharwad, Karnataka. I'm pursuing Bachelor's of technology (B.Tech) in Electrical Engineering.

My research interests include Computer Architecture, Memory Systems, Formal Verification(Runtime Verification), Digital Systems, Hardware Security Embedded Systems and Compilers. I am currently working on Runtime Verification (Design and Development of Runtime monitor processors), Hardware Prefetching and Branch Prediction Techniques and Analysing cloud workloads to improve energy efficiency

I have worked as a research intern at IIT Dharwad under the guidance of Prof. Rajshekar K where I worked on designing and evaluating Task Scheduling Algorithms for Heterogeneous Secure Systems (HSS) which dealt with secure acceleration of neural network applications against Hardware Trojans through assisted parallelism.
